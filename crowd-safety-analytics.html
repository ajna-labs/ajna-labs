About Crowd Safety Analytics

Ensure the safety of your place with less human intervention and remote analysis in real time. Analyze, track, report and get alerts from our crowd safety analysis system that will track the crowd

The system will be highly responsive and work in real-time to provide the updates to you in the web dashboard.

  • Mask Detection
  • Social Distancing
  • Contact Tracing
  • Safety Reporting
  • Crowd Density Analysis
  • Risk Prediction

Implementation

The implementation process will be customized to provide you the best and most accurate solution. Our team will survey the area that requires analytics before installation of our hardware and software. The hardware once setup will be automatically providing you the updates and can be viewed in our web dashboard.

  • What type of cameras does it work with?

    Our product is a plug-and-play model and it is compatible with existing in-house CCTV cameras.

  • How can you view the analytics and data?

    The data can be viewed using our web dashboard which can be deployed on your server or stay in our server.
